# TicTacToe_with_cpp
this is a simple tic tac toe game build with c++, 
to play this game you have to enter the the prefered number from the number pad cordinate
1 > top left
2 > top middle
3 > top right
4 > middle left
5 > middle middle
6 > middle right
7 > bottom left
8 > bottom middle
9 > bottom right
